IroGuide Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

Please report suspected security vulnerabilities privately to support@iroguide.com.
Include the affected URL or component, reproduction steps, impact, and any supporting evidence. Do not include secrets or personal data that are not needed to reproduce the issue.

We ask researchers to avoid privacy violations, service disruption, social engineering, denial-of-service testing, automated traffic that degrades the service, and accessing or modifying data that does not belong to them. Stop testing and report the issue if you encounter user data.

We will acknowledge a report as soon as practical, investigate it, and coordinate remediation and disclosure timing with the reporter. We will not pursue legal action against good-faith research that follows this policy.
